About Lukuo


Lukuo is an African media recommender that helps you predict media (movies, music, tv-series) you are likely to enjoy and help you avoid those you won't, using a filtering engine together with data provided. Lukuo is free and only seeks to improve user's media choice. The usage of this website is guarded by these terms.
